Our Business model

Strategic Business Units (SBUs) are organized based on customer segment who sign up for training with SiS Inflexionpoint BD Ltd. Each SBU has a business head whose responsibility is to drive the P&L and he/she is responsible for the growth of their unit Assessments and content development across SBUs are handled by the Learning services group.

  • Individual Learning Solutions (ILS)
  • Enterprise Learning Solutions (ELS)
  • Global Learning Solutions (GLS)
  • IIHT Cloud Solutions (ICS)
