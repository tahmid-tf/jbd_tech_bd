About Us

SiS Inflexionpoint (BD) Ltd. (SiSIP) is Bangladesh's most significant management consulting, technology services, and outsourcing firm. With our in-depth knowledge of several business verticals and extensive resources, we deploy the relevant people, skills, and technology to assist firms in improving their performance and transforming their cost burdens in this competitive market. SiSIP specializes in end-to-end information technology solutions, including custom software development, server and data centre infrastructure management, network security, enterprise software, hardware, devices, industrial automation and robotics, and media and marketing. SiSIP began its journey to provide a one-stop-shop for our clients, bringing together all of our wings and experience under one roof.

SISIP began operations in 2014 with the mission of providing end-to-end IT infrastructure services, world-class hardware solutions, industry-leading software solutions, and world-class IT training to simplify and streamline the deployment, management, and upgrade of IT infrastructure in all types of organizations in Bangladesh.

We have over two decades of local expertise in the supply and implementation of IT hardware, channel marketing and distribution, system integration, customized software development, data centre and Internet of Things device creation, training, outsourcing, and other technical services. We provide full-stack software development skills through our network of technology enthusiasts, Industry Certified ICT trainers, and a service centre equipped with various OEM- certified tools and facilities. SiSIP takes pleasure in offering our clients creative, personalized, and practical solutions. We believe in assisting clients by delivering exceptional performance and establishing long-term partnerships via responsiveness and relevance, as well as by continually providing value. Our innovative and creative solutions exceed customers' expectations by achieving their commercial objectives and adhering strictly to the ethical principles of information technology, engineering, and media communications.

As a provider of software development services, we endeavour to give our customers high- quality application development services that enable them to stay ahead of the competition. We are professionals in leveraging technology to increase corporate efficiency and competitive advantage via custom-designed features.

By utilizing standardized techniques and established IT processes, we can decrease offshore software outsourcing projects' complexity, risk, and expense. We assist our clients in developing bespoke software applications and helping them throughout the software development life cycle, including project management, systems analysis and design, development, installation, training, and application maintenance. We are real game enthusiasts! We create cross-platform games, which means they can run on iOS, Android, Windows, Play Station, or Xbox (Completely Platform Independent). Our team possesses advanced 2D and 3D game creation talents in Cocos2D, Box2D, Unity3D, Unreal, HTML5, and FLASH.

Our team of highly skilled individuals is available to help you get your app ideas to the market. Our talented staff can develop apps that are artistically designed, visually attractive, completely functional, and business-oriented for their intended audience. Our developers create diverse, business-centric apps using development tools such as the Android SDK and the newest Android Studio. We've developed applications for Android Marshmallow, Nougat, and Oreo and have experience and competence in doing so.

Our IoT team is composed of business and engineering specialists that provide a range of embedded engineering and automation services and consulting and product manufacturing services that use artificial intelligence. Our emphasis is not on engineering miracles but on straightforward technology answers to perplexing and persistent issues. We believe that simplicity is the pinnacle of sophistication.
