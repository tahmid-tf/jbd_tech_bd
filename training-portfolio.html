Training

From its inception, we have conducted multiple training programs on various technologies to our customers. To see the growing demands on the training requirement from various customers and as well as our partners, we have tied up with one of the Asia’s leading ICT training organization IIHT to bring world class, up to date and top quality training facility to Bangladesh. SiS has become the master franchise of IIHT, India in 2018. Our training facility equipped with state of the art fully air-conditioned class rooms, latest Dell PCs/laptop/servers/ storage devices, multimedia equipment, physical labs and virtual learning fabric. We are capable of conducting training for500+ students in multiple batches.

SBU

Our Business Model

  • Strategic Business Units (SBUs) are organized based on customer segment who sign up for training with IIHT.
  • Each SBU has a business head whose responsibility is to drive the P&L and he/she is responsible for the growth of their unit.
  • Assessments and content development across SBUs are handled by the Learning services group.

Enterprise Learning Services (ELS)

ELS is not limited to only training, it extend the service from consultancy, managed services, work force optimization and learning as a service.

Advantage of I-topology

IIHT has the world’s first virtualized “Infrastructure as a Service” catering to

  • IT Training Industry
  • Software Verification and Validation Industry

I-topology eliminates need for large & complex infrastructure, Make the learning simple & affordable with the help of official curriculum certified lab infrastructure. IIHT and SISIP can ensure the below facilities through i-topology.

  • ‘Own Virtual Data Centre’ with flexibility & control of real data centers Preconfigured Stack of IT solutions for on-demand quick provisioning
  • Design and provision multi-site data centre on the fly from same console Control network Bandwidth and traffic flow policies between virtual sites
  • Preserving knowledge, sharing and reusing it instantaneously
  • Official learning curriculum infrastructure of respective software vendor
